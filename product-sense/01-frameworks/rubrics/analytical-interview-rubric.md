# Analytical Interview Rubric

## Three Evaluation Areas

Analytical interviews usually evaluate:

- Analytical skills
- Critical thinking
- Culture fit

## Core Signals

Analytical skills:

- Data literacy
- Comfort with metrics

Critical thinking:

- Ability to diagnose problems
- Ability to prioritize
- Ability to execute a plan

Culture fit:

- Communication
- Collaboration
- Curiosity

## What Strong Looks Like

Strong candidates:

- Use data effectively
- Ask good clarifying questions
- Make solid assumptions
- Choose metrics with clear pros and cons
- Scope solvable problems
- Prioritize by urgency, cost/benefit, and testability
- Explain executable plans
- Communicate their thought process clearly
- Involve the interviewer throughout
- Show genuine curiosity about root causes

## Self-Check Before Answering

Ask yourself:

- Did I use data, not just intuition?
- Did I choose a metric that fits the problem?
- Did I diagnose before solving?
- Did I prioritize what to investigate or build first?
- Did I explain how to execute the plan?
- Did I communicate clearly and check in?
- Did I collaborate with the interviewer?
- Did I show curiosity about why the problem is happening?
