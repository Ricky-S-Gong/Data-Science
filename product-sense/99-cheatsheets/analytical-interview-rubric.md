# Analytical Interview Rubric

## What Gets Scored

- Data literacy
- Comfort with metrics
- Diagnosis
- Prioritization
- Execution
- Communication
- Collaboration
- Curiosity

## Very Strong Signals

- Extract valuable insights from data.
- Identify key patterns and reports/tests to run.
- Discuss metrics thoroughly and accurately.
- Recommend ways to streamline analysis.
- Ask insightful questions.
- Set up a clear test plan.
- Prioritize maximum benefit with minimal cost.
- Account for KPIs, bottlenecks, potential errors, and alternatives.
- Communicate proactively and check in throughout.
- Make the conversation collaborative.
- Ask questions that get to the root of the issue.

## Interview Reminder

Do not rush into an answer. Diagnose, scope, prioritize, and explain your plan. Treat the interviewer as a collaborator and keep them with you.
