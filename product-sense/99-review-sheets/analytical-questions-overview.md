# Analytical Questions Overview

## Core Idea

Analytical PM questions test structured thinking with data in a product context.

中文理解：分析类 PM 题不是单纯考数据或计算，而是考你能否在产品场景中，用结构化方式拆问题、定目标、选指标、做判断。

PM = Product Manager，产品经理。

## What To Show

- Clear problem breakdown
- Explicit goal
- Relevant metrics
- Logical assumptions
- Product and user context
- Tradeoffs and next steps

## Four Common Types

### Metrics

Purpose: choose the right metrics to evaluate product health or success.

Key terms:

- Metrics: 指标
- North star metric: 北极星指标，最能代表长期用户价值和产品方向的核心指标
- Guardrail metrics: 护栏指标，用来防止某个局部指标优化伤害整体体验或业务健康

Examples:

- Lyft app health metrics
- Airbnb north star metric
- Define success metrics

What to emphasize:

- Goal
- User journey
- North star metric
- Input metrics
- Guardrail metrics

### A/B Testing

Purpose: design and interpret experiments.

A/B test = A/B testing，对照实验。通常把用户分成 control group 和 treatment group，比较两个或多个方案的效果。

Examples:

- Increase Google search queries
- Reduce frustration in Google Maps
- Test Lyft dynamic pricing

What to emphasize:

- Hypothesis
- Experiment design
- Success metrics
- Guardrail metrics
- Result interpretation
- Next steps

### Estimation

Purpose: break down ambiguity and estimate scale.

TAM = Total Addressable Market，总可服务市场规模。

Examples:

- Driverless car market size
- Slack TAM
- YouTube daily operating cost

What to emphasize:

- Structure
- Assumptions
- Simple math
- Sanity check
- Business relevance

### Execution

Purpose: diagnose and solve product problems.

DAU = Daily Active Users，日活跃用户数。

Examples:

- Facebook friend requests down 10%
- Messenger DAU down
- Food delivery time increased
- Stripe merchant fraud increased

What to emphasize:

- Clarify metric and scope
- Segment users/product/platform/time
- Check data quality
- Identify root causes
- Prioritize investigation
- Recommend actions

## Interview Reminder

Do not answer analytical questions as isolated data puzzles. Always connect the analysis back to the product, users, business goal, and final recommendation.
